Recipe #1: Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 1 tbsp

Step 1: Mix all ingredients together.
Step 2: Pour batter into a hot pan.
Step 3: Cook until golden brown.
